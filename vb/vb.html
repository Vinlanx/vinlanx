Velocity Blur

Speed. Momentum. Focus.

Velocity Blur is a small client-side mod that adds a dynamic velocity blur effect based on your movement speed. Experience speed like never before.

Client-Side Only

This mod works entirely on the client. You do not need to install it on the server.

Download Mod

Mod Overview

Enhance your gameplay immersion with dynamic visuals. The faster you move, the stronger the blur effect becomes, giving you a true sense of velocity.

Perfect for parkour, elytra flying, or simply sprinting through your world.

System Features

Visual Mechanics

  • Dynamic Blur: Motion blur that scales automatically with player speed.
  • Immersive FX: Subtle effects that don't obstruct gameplay but enhance the feeling of speed.

HUD Elements

  • Speedometer: Simple speed counter in the top-right corner.
  • Metrics: Displays speed in blocks/s.
  • Optional: Can be toggled off in config.

Compatibility

  • Client-Side: Safe for multiplayer servers.
  • Performance: Lightweight implementation with minimal FPS impact.

Configuration

Customize your experience through the mod settings.

You can adjust the blur intensity, toggle the speed HUD, and fine-tune when the effect kicks in.

Tip: Access the config menu via the Mod Menu interface.

F.A.Q.

Q: Do I need this on the server?
A: No. Velocity Blur is strictly a client-side mod. It works on any server.
Q: Does it cause lag?
A: The impact is minimal on most modern systems. It uses efficient rendering techniques.
Q: Can I turn off the numbers?
A: Yes, the speed HUD (blocks per second) is optional and can be disabled in the config.
