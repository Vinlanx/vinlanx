Operational Capabilities

Current Version: 0.1.7.7 Alpha

Dynamic Sound

  • Physics: Sound travels at realistic speeds. Flash first, boom later.
  • Distance: From deafening roars to muffled echoes up to 5km away.
  • Environment: Cave explosions echo loudly; surface sounds are muffled indoors.
  • 7 Power Tiers: From small creepers to apocalyptic detonations.

Visual Effects

  • Pyrotechnics: Dynamic fireballs, plasma sparks, and cooling smoke.
  • Atmosphere: Electrical discharge sparks and ground dust clouds.
  • Dynamic Flash: Screen flashes scale with power and visibility.

Tangible Impact

  • Shockwaves: Camera shakes and physical player push in enclosed spaces.
  • Destruction: Cave-ins, falling dripstone, and realistic craters.
  • Interference: Explosions cause Redstone lamps to flicker and spark.

Troubleshooting

Crash Prevention

WARNING: Explosions >50 power may be unstable.

  • Forge Version: Ensure you are using 47.4.0.
  • OptiFine: Highly incompatible. Use Oculus + Embeddium instead.
  • Conflicts: Test without other mods first to identify conflicts.

Critical Intel

Black Textures / StitcherException Fix

If you see black textures or crash with a StitcherException, follow these steps:

  1. Open the ExplosionOverhaul.jar with WinRar or 7-Zip.
  2. Navigate to: \assets\explosionoverhaul\textures\particle
  3. Delete contents in folders sglow, glow, glow_2, BUT KEEP the 64x folders inside them.
  4. Launch Game ‚Üí Mods ‚Üí Explosion Overhaul ‚Üí Config ‚Üí Render.
  5. Select 64x textures.
