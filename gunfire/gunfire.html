An add-on for many weapon mods that adds realistic distant gunfire and artillery sounds, creating echo effects and a deeply immersive battlefield atmosphere.

Mission Briefing

Gunfire Overhaul: Echoes of the Battlefield is both a standalone mod and an integration add-on. It completely rethinks the soundscape of combat in Minecraft.

As a standalone mod, it fills the world with ambient battle sounds. As an add-on, it integrates with popular weapon mods, replacing standard gunshot sounds with dynamic and realistic ones that change depending on distance, caliber, and environment.

Operational Capabilities

Current Version: 0.0.6 Alpha

Dynamic Sound

  • Distance Reality: From a loud shot up close to a distant, muffled echo up to 3 kilometers away.
  • Caliber Physics: A pistol shot and a sniper rifle shot sound completely different with unique audio profiles.
  • Speed of Sound: Visuals first, audio later. Sound waves travel at realistic speeds.

Battlefield Atmosphere

  • Ambient Warfare: Random gunshot sounds are generated around the player, simulating a nearby conflict.
  • Audio Scenarios: Everything from single shots to short bursts and sustained automatic fire.
  • Weapon Variety: Simulation of varying power, from pistols to heavy machine guns.

Troubleshooting

Critical Errors / Crashes

If you experience crashes upon launch:

  • Check Forge Version: Ensure you are using the recommended Forge build for your MC version (47.4.0)
  • Mod Conflicts: Try running the game with only Gunfire Overhaul and its dependencies. Add other mods back one by one to identify conflicts.
